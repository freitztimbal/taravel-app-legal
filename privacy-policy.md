# Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 1, 2026

## 1. Information We Collect

### Account Information
- Email address
- Authentication data

### User Generated Data
- Trip names
- Destinations
- Trip dates
- Photos uploaded for trip covers
- Invitation lists

## 2. How We Use Your Information
We use collected data to:
- Provide travel planning functionality
- Enable social trip collaboration
- Send notifications
- Maintain account security

## 3. Photo Storage
Photos uploaded by users are stored in cloud storage services.

We may:
- Compress images for performance
- Generate secure access URLs

Do not upload sensitive personal photos or confidential documents.

## 4. Third Party Services
We may use:
- Authentication providers
- Cloud storage providers
- Notification services

These services may process your data.

## 5. Data Security
We use:
- Secure authentication
- Access control policies
- Encrypted connections where possible

However, no system is completely secure.

## 6. Data Retention
We may retain data until:
- User requests deletion
- Account is terminated

## 7. Account Deletion
Users may request deletion by contacting support.

Upon deletion:
- Personal data will be removed where technically possible.

## 8. International Users
By using the service, you agree your data may be processed globally.

## 9. Children’s Privacy
Users must meet minimum legal age requirements.

## 10. Changes to Privacy Policy
We may update this policy periodically.
